About me

Hi my Name is Ellie, and the only thing I love more than cooking is getting to eat and take photos of the food I cooked

I'm an Indiana girl and I love home style cooking but I also love to use strong flavors with unique ingredient combinations like my strawberry Spinach Quesadilas with walnut balsamic dressing and feta cheese. Cooking has always been a big part of my family life and holiday traditions, and 4-H gave me an early love for trying out new recipes in the kitchen

The purpose of this website is to act as a hub for my family to be able to store and share our favorite recipes. After years of recipes being passed down and finding new recipes online, keeping them organized and accesible has been a challenge. With this site, we can easily find our most loved recipes whenever needed!
